Frequently Asked Question's

What is FlexDrive?

FlexDrive is an innovative car rental service that offers subscription-based access to a diverse fleet of vehicles, providing a convenient and flexible alternative to traditional car ownership.

How does FlexDrive's subscription model work?

FlexDrive's subscription model allows you to choose a subscription plan that suits your needs. With a monthly fee, you gain access to a range of vehicles, covering maintenance, insurance, and more, all without the commitments of ownership.

What's included in the subscription fee?

The subscription fee covers a comprehensive package, including maintenance, insurance, and even vehicle depreciation. This means you can enjoy your chosen vehicle without worrying about the typical costs associated with ownership.

Is there a long-term commitment with FlexDrive?

No, FlexDrive offers commitment-free subscription options. There are no long-term contracts, giving you the freedom to subscribe for the duration that suits you best, whether it's for a month or longer.

What types of vehicles are available in the FlexDrive fleet?

FlexDrive offers a diverse fleet, including sleek sedans, spacious SUVs, eco-conscious electric cars, and more. This variety ensures that there's a suitable vehicle for every subscriber.
